**Plan Issues**
1. Critical — Step 1 / Step 3: the proposed `EditorBlockManager` only changes when final Soniox tokens arrive, but the specs require it to stay aligned with user typing, inline edits, clear actions, and session lifecycle. In the current code, the only editor coordination point is `registerEditor` in [Editor.tsx](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/src/renderer/overlay/editor/Editor.tsx#L16) and `OverlayContext` clears the Lexical document directly in [OverlayContext.tsx](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/src/renderer/overlay/OverlayContext.tsx#L32), so the manager described in [3-plan.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/.mound/tasks/13/3-plan.md#L24) would drift out of sync as soon as the user types, edits, or clears. That conflicts with the ownership and block-boundary rules in [spec/models.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/spec/models.md#L46), [spec/features/inline-typing.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/spec/features/inline-typing.md#L35), and [spec/features/inline-editing.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/spec/features/inline-editing.md#L16). Fix: move block ownership/state into a higher-level controller that also observes Lexical user edits and clear/session events, or explicitly add those synchronization steps to the plan.

2. Major — Step 2 / Step 3: the undo-history design is not verified and is internally inconsistent. [3-plan.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/.mound/tasks/13/3-plan.md#L133) walks through several contradictory approaches, then settles on mutating `historyState.undoStack` immediately after `editor.update()` at [3-plan.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/.mound/tasks/13/3-plan.md#L224), while also acknowledging a race at [3-plan.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/.mound/tasks/13/3-plan.md#L284). The current editor uses the stock [HistoryPlugin](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/src/renderer/overlay/editor/Editor.tsx#L35), and nothing in the repo verifies that direct stack mutation is a supported contract. Fix: add a proof step first: confirm the actual Lexical API/version behavior with a focused test or spike, then update the plan to one concrete, validated approach.

3. Major — Step 3 / Risk 5: the lifecycle assumption is wrong. The plan says a fresh block manager on editor remount “aligns with the session lifecycle” and treats `onShow: "append"` as out of scope at [3-plan.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/.mound/tasks/13/3-plan.md#L290), but the overlay window is hidden, not destroyed, in [session.ts](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/src/main/session.ts#L192), and the specs require `fresh` vs `append` behavior on show in [spec/features/system-tray.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/spec/features/system-tray.md#L47) and [spec/features/text-output.md](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/spec/features/text-output.md#L26). The renderer currently has no `onSessionStart` handling at all, despite it being exposed in [preload.d.ts](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-12-bf191077/src/shared/preload.d.ts#L13). Fix: add explicit lifecycle wiring for `session:start` and `onShow`, including when to clear or preserve both Lexical content and block state.
