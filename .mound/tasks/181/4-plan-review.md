[tokenMerger.ts](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-2-8c6c1b01/src/renderer/overlay/editor/tokenMerger.ts#L36) and [TokenCommitPlugin.tsx](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-2-8c6c1b01/src/renderer/overlay/editor/TokenCommitPlugin.tsx#L193) show that the renderer currently has no semantic “utterance ended” signal, while [soniox.ts](C:/code/draftable/stex/.mound/worktrees/worker-2-8c6c1b01/src/main/soniox.ts#L154) already sees Soniox endpoint markers before filtering them out.
