## What each test covers

1. **retrieves all recorded audio after pause** — Pushes 5 tagged chunks, slices from 0, verifies all data present in order
2. **retrieves audio from the middle of buffered data** — Slices from chunk boundary mid-stream, verifies only suffix returned
3. **retrieves audio from a mid-chunk timestamp** — Slices at timestamp falling inside a chunk, verifies correct containing chunk returned
4. **retrieves correct data after wrap-around eviction** — Overfills buffer to trigger eviction, verifies surviving chunks have correct tags in order
5. **retrieves from mid-point after wrap-around** — Slices from middle of surviving range after eviction, verifies correct data
6. **supports clear-then-record cycle (multiple sessions)** — Records, clears, records again, verifies session isolation
